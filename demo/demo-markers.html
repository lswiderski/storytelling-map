World Cities Tour

This tour showcases major world cities with custom-styled markers. Each marker has been customized with different colors and properties to demonstrate the flexibility of the storytelling map system.

Tokyo, Japan

Tokyo, officially Tokyo Metropolis, is the capital of Japan and one of the most populated urban areas in the world. The city is known for its modern architecture, cutting-edge technology, and rich cultural traditions.

This marker is styled in red and has a minimum zoom level of 5, preventing excessive zoom-out.

Sydney, Australia

Sydney is the capital city of New South Wales and the most populous city in Australia. Famous for its harbourfront Sydney Opera House and the iconic Harbour Bridge.

This marker is styled in bright green with standard zoom behavior.

Dubai, UAE

Dubai is a city in the United Arab Emirates known for luxury shopping, ultramodern architecture, and a lively nightlife scene. The Burj Khalifa, an 830m-tall tower, dominates the skyscraper-filled skyline.

This marker is styled in gold to represent the city's luxury and wealth.

New York City, USA

New York City comprises five boroughs sitting where the Hudson River meets the Atlantic Ocean. At its core is Manhattan, a densely populated borough that's among the world's major commercial, financial and cultural centers.

This marker is styled in blue and has a maximum zoom level of 13, preventing excessive zoom-in.

Cape Town, South Africa

Cape Town is a port city on South Africa's southwest coast, on a peninsula beneath the imposing Table Mountain. The city is known for its harbor, natural setting, and well-known landmarks such as Table Mountain and Cape Point.

This marker is styled in magenta with custom tooltip and popup text.

Antarctica

Antarctica is Earth's southernmost continent, containing the geographic South Pole. It is covered by ice with an average thickness of 1.9 km.

Note: This location has a hidden marker - no marker appears on the map, but the map still navigates to this location when you scroll here.
